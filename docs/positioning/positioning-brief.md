# Positioning Brief

## One-liner
OpenField is a research operations platform for field data collection.

## Who it is for
- NGOs running surveys and baselines
- Academic research teams
- Government or health programs collecting field data

## Core differentiation
- Project-first structure
- Enumerator assignments without login
- Ethics controls (consent, attestation, restricted exports)
- Supervisor-grade QA

## Proof points
- Export-ready data
- Assignment-scoped links
- QA alerts and error logs
